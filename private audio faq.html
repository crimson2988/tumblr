What are Collection audios?

Collection audios are all the audios by one person put into a single audio file. The audio plays sequentially with the audios playing one after the other.

What are Compilation audios?

Copmilation audios contain multiple audios that play one after the other. They are typically made of audios from different people. They also typically have a particular theme.

What are Mix or Mashup audios?

Mix audios are two or more audios put together such that they play at the same time.
